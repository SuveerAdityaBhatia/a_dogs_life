About Us

We are an independent body, however, we have been accredited by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports and we are recognized as a part of the effort being made to keep the youth of the nation engaged. We started this academy to make the youth completely fall in love with the sport and to let them know that if mastered, it can turn into a career. Football is an art form that requires one to focus and most importantly have fun. We realized how big of an impact playing a sport can make in one’s life and we want to carry this realization forward and educate the impressionable youth that they can become whatever they want but it's them who need to make it happen for themselves. No one else can achieve their dream for them, they have to do it on their own. We formed this academy because football has changed our lives in positive ways. We have played at school, college, state, and international level football so we feel that with our collective experience, we are most suited to give the youth advice on how to turn an interest into a proper future. We strive to give the youth the best chance at achieving their dreams.
