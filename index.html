About Us

A dog's life was found in 2009 and has groomed dogs of many breeds ever since. With our barber having more than 20 years of experience, we ensure that your beloved pet is in good hands. Our shop's well-lit interior and colors that are easy on the eyes make sure that your pet does not feel uneasy while waiting for his turn. We are preferred by many families in Chicago to take care of their pets while they are out on a vacation.
